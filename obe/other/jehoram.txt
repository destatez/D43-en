====== Jehoram ======

===== Facts: =====

Jehoram was the name of two different kings in the Old Testament.

  * One King Jehoram ruled over the kingdom of Judah for eight years. He was the son of King Jehoshaphat.
  * The other King Jehoram, also known as "Joram," ruled over the kingdom of Israel for twelve years. He was the son of King Ahab.
  * Both Jehoram and Joram reigned during the time that the prophet Obadiah was prophesying.
