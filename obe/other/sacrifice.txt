====== sacrifice, offering ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the terms "sacrifice" and "offering" refer to special gifts given to God as an act of worshiping him.

  * The word "offering" generally refers to anything that is offered or given. Offerings to God were specific things that he commanded the Israelites to do to express devotion and obedience to him. that God required the Israelites to give
  * The names of the different offerings, such as "burnt offering" and "peace offering," indicated what kind of offering was being given.
  * The term "sacrifice" refers to anything that is given or done at great cost to the giver.
  * Sacrifices to God often involved the killing of an animal.  * Only the sacrifice of Jesus, God's perfect, sinless Son, can completely cleanse people from sin. Animal sacrifices could never do that.
  * People who worshiped idols also offered sacrifices to their false gods.
  * The figurative expression "offer yourselves as a living sacrifice" means, "live your life in complete obedience to God, giving up everything in order to serve him."


===== Translation Suggestions =====

  * The term "offering" could also be translated as "a gift to God" or "something given to God" or "something valuable that is presented to God."
  * The term "sacrifice" can be translated as "kill an animal and give it to God" or "special animal killed and presented to God" or "killed animal or person for giving to an idol," depending on the context.
