====== tent of meeting ======


===== Facts: =====

When God gave his laws at Mount Sinai, he told the Israelites to make the tent of meeting. This was the holy place where he would meet with them..

  * The tent of meeting was like a house made of cloth stretched over a wooden framework. 
  * In the Bible the tent of meeting was the place where Moses met with God before the tabernacle was established.
  * Exodus says that the tent of meeting was set up outside the camp of the Israelites. 
  * When Moses went to the tent of meeting a pillar of cloud would appear revealing the presence of God at the tent. 
  * Sometimes in the Bible tent of meeting and tabernacle are used to speak about the same place.
